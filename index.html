About the equation

MM‑VCM inverse equation (closed‑form back‑calculation):

\[ N_0 = \frac{K \cdot N_t \cdot e^{k \cdot dt}}{N_t \cdot e^{k \cdot dt} + (K - N_t \cdot e^{k \cdot dt}) \cdot e^{r(T) \cdot dt}} \]

with \[ dt = \max(0, t - \lambda) \], \[ r(T) = \frac{\ln(2)}{G_{\text{base}}} \cdot \exp\left( -\frac{(T-37)^2}{2\sigma^2} \right) \].

Clinical notes & safeguards
  • Set dt to zero when t ≤ λ; then N₀ = Nt.
  • Defaults reflect organism–matrix literature ranges; manual override is supported for local validation.
  • No clamping applied—raw formula output shown (may be <0 in edge cases).

Quick guidance

1) Choose the organism–matrix profile and apply defaults. 2) Enter Nt, delay, and temperature. 3) Optionally override λ, k, K, Gbase, σ to match local data. 4) Click Compute to obtain N₀ and diagnostic terms dt and r(T). For extended delays or uncertain T, report intervals in clinical documentation.
MM‑VCM is intended to assist with delay-corrected interpretation of CFU counts; use alongside clinical judgment and local laboratory validation.
